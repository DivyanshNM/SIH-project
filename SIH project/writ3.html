Quo warranto

Challenges an individual's right to hold a public office or position. If the court finds that the individual is not lawfully entitled to the position, it can remove them from office and potentially order a new appointment or election to fill the position warranto serves as a mechanism to ensure that public offices are held by individuals who are properly qualified and legally entitled to serve
