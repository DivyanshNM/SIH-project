Article 16: Equality of Opportunity

There shall be equality of opportunity for all citizens in matters relating to employment or appointment to any office under the State.

Article 16 guarantees that when it comes to getting a government job, everyone should have an equal chance. The government cannot refuse to hire someone because of their religion, caste, gender, or where they come from. However, this article also allows the government to help groups that have been left behind by offering them reservations.
