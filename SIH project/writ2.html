Mandamus

Commands a government official or agency to perform a duty that they are legally obligated to complete. This writ is used when a petitioner believes that a public official or agency has failed to fulfill a legal obligation or duty Mandamus is not used to compel a discretionary action, but rather to enforce actions that are mandatory and not subject to the official's discretion.
