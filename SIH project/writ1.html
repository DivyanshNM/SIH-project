Habeas Corpus

Habeas Corpus is a legal principle that protects individuals from unlawful detention. The primary function of habeas corpus is to prevent arbitrary imprisonment and ensure that a person cannot be held without just cause. It guarantees the right to a fair trial and due process. If a court finds that the detention is unlawful, it can order the release of the detainee
